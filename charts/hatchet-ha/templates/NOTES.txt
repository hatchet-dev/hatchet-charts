Running an HA deployment on single-instance datastores defeats the purpose. These
are intended for DEVELOPMENT and STAGING only. For production, run your own
PostgreSQL and RabbitMQ (a managed service or a deployment you operate end-to-end)
and disable the bundled ones:

  helm upgrade {{ .Release.Name }} hatchet/hatchet-ha \
    --set postgres.enabled=false \
    --set rabbitmq.enabled=false \
    --set sharedConfig.env.DATABASE_URL='postgres://user:pass@host:5432/hatchet?sslmode=require' \
    --set sharedConfig.env.SERVER_MSGQUEUE_RABBITMQ_URL='amqp://user:pass@host:5672/'

Details: https://github.com/hatchet-dev/hatchet-charts/blob/main/charts/hatchet-ha/README.md#bundled-postgresql--rabbitmq
